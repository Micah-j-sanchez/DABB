About D:DAB:B

D:DAB:B (Data:about:blank) is a safer, offline variant of DAB:B meant to convert an about:blank launcher into a data URL to further evade censorship. Our mission is to provide users with enhanced protection against URL blocklists, allowing them to access their favorite content without limitations or restrictions.

How to use:

To start using D:DAB:B, simply go to (this domain)/ddabb.html?url=(desired URL). Experience the freedom of browsing without restrictions and unlock content blocked by traditional censorship measures.
