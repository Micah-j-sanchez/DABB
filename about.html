About DAB:B

DAB:B (Dynamic about blank) is a simple and innovative platform designed to bypass URL blocklists and censorship. Our mission is to provide users with unrestricted access to the web, ensuring that they can freely explore their favorite content without limitations or restrictions.

How to use:

To start, simply go to https://v-0-i-d-js.github.io/DABB?url=(desired url)
